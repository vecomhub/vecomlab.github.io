Do You Work With Cryptocurrency?

Yes, we have a partnership agreement with Binance Pay, enabling us to accept cryptocurrency payments from our clients.This is completely legitimate as we have a signed agreement in place.

However, the process is neither simple nor quick. To utilize Binance Pay, you would need to register a corporate account with Binance and undergo various verification procedures.

Subsequently, you can request access to Binance Pay, which entails additional scrutiny. Our own experience took over 4 months to complete. It is a lengthy and demanding process, but the results are undoubtedly worth it.

It contributes to our collection of exceptional experiences.
